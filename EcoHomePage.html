About EcoThreads

Founded in 2001 by Entrepreneur Nathaniel Ambrosio, Kirk Dominique Catapang, Antonio Miguel Villanueva, and Aaron Recana. At EcoThreads, we believe that clothing is like a relic passed on from era to era. Every piece has a story, and our mission is to give these items a second life, making sustainable fashion accessible while connecting the past with the present.

Our Goals

EcoThreads aims to make thrifting easier and more enjoyable by helping customers browse and reserve items online, organizing inventory efficiently, tracking stock in real-time, and supporting staff with faster, more accurate management.

Our Vision

To make EcoThreads the go-to online and in-store destination for second-hand fashion by combining sustainability with convenience, efficiency, and customer satisfaction—giving every item a second chance while making thrifting simple, organized, and enjoyable for everyone.
